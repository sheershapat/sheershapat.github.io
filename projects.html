Projects

  • Pong

    The objective of this game is to try to score as many points as possible by hitting the ball with the paddle before it touches the floor.

  • Flappy Bird

    Click the mouse to begin the game. The bird will automatically drop, so to fly up, press the mouse. Try to avoid the obstacles. After you pass each obstacle, your score goes up by one.

  • Snake Game

    Press the arrow keys to move and try to collect the green "apples". Each time you collect an apple your snake will increase in size. Avoid running into the edges and yourself.
